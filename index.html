Welcome to my page.
My research explores how decentralized groups in conflict and post-conflict regions navigate collective action without a central authority. My current work focuses on the Southeast Asia, especially post-coup Myanmar. I ask how decentralized collective sustain agency, take risks in for public goods, and limit free-riding without external enforcement. I use qualitative and experimental methods to understand how diverse groups overcome these challenges during conflict.
